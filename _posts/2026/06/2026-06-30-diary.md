Docker イメージを順次構築：

```bash
declare -a projs=(
    shinjuku-central
    shinjuku-yotsuya
    shinjuku-tsurumaki
    shinjuku-nishiochiai
    shinjuku-toyama
    shinjuku-kitashinjuku
    shinjuku-nakamachi
    shinjuku-tsunohazu
    shinjuku-okubo
    shinjuku-shimoochiai
)
for dir in "${projs[@]}"; do
  pushd "$dir"
  make -n -f ../Makefile -f ../Makefile.vars DOCKER_FILE=../Dockerfile docker-build
  popd
done
```

GNU `make` (1) の対象を {docker-push,clean} に代えてもう一ループ。
